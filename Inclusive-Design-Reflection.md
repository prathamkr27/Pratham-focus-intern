 1 Vulnerable Populations & Challenges

 ADHD: Executive dysfunction causes "time blindness," task paralysis, hyperfocus/distractions , overwhelm from cluttered apps , these devices are usually designed to be high dopamine .

 Autism: Sensory overload, rigid routines broken by poor UX, navigation struggles; need visual timers/simple interfaces

2 UX Communication Styles 

Avoid: Dense text, buried settings, unpredictable notifications .

Support: One-click tasks, high-contrast visuals, customizable alerts, voice commands, pie charts for progress 

Team style: Clear visual deadlines , async/flexible check-ins, simple language, body-doubling options.
