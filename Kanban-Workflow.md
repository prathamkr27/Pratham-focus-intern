Identify one way you can improve task tracking in your role.
When building data pipelines or preparing datasets, tasks often involve long run-times or sequential dependencies (e.g., you cannot format the JSONL file until the extraction script finishes running). By using a Kanban board, I will break large assignments into smaller, distinct cards and move them through specific columns like "Data Gathering," "Scripting," "Awaiting Compute/Run Time," and "Ready for Review."
