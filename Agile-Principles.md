Q3 Summarize the main differences between Scrum and Kanban in your own words.

Scrum is built around discipline and commitment. The team agrees to a specific amount of work they will finish within a set timeframe (usually 2 weeks), called a Sprint.
Kanban is built around flexibility and efficiency. There are no "sprints" or start/end dates. Work is a constant stream, and you only move to the next task when you have the capacity.
