Q1 Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

ans 1 Focus Bear was created because the CEO, Jeremy Nagel, struggled with his own ADHD and found that existing apps weren't "forceful" enough to stop him from doomscrolling. I read on the "About Us" page how he built this to blend habit guidance with distraction blocking because he needed a system that "did the habits with him" rather than just sending a notification he could swipe away.



Q2 What problem is Focus Bear solving?

Ans2 It solves the "execution gap" for neurodivergent people. On the "Science" page, it explains how it moves users from impulsive thinking to deliberate thinking. It specifically targets Executive Dysfunction the struggle to actually start a task even when you know you should—by using "Grizzly Bear" mode to hard-block distractions across all devices simultaneously.

Q3 Why do you think this mission is important?

Ans3 This mission is vital because, as stated on their Monash Innovation feature page, "Adults with ADHD are up to 40% less productive than neurotypical peers." Providing an affordable, science-backed alternative to expensive coaching makes productivity accessible. A line that stuck with me from their Manifesto was: "Focus shouldn't hurt." It reframes productivity from a chore into something supportive.
